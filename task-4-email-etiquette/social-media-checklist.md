# 🛡️ Social Media Strategy: The Professional Checklist
**Part B – Digital Presence & Ethics Guide**

---

### ✅ The "Do's" (Optimization & Growth)
| Category | Action Plan |
| :--- | :--- |
| **👤 Professionalism** | **Optimize your LinkedIn:** Use a clean headshot and clearly state your **B.Tech (AI/ML)** focus in your headline. |
| **🔒 Privacy** | **Audit Settings:** Review privacy configurations regularly to maintain a clear boundary between your professional and personal life. |
| **🤝 Networking** | **Engage Early:** Follow industry leaders and contribute actively to tech communities on **GitHub** and **Stack Overflow**. |
| **⚖️ Ethics** | **Fact-Check:** Verify all information before sharing to prevent the spread of digital misinformation. |
| **🛡️ Security** | **Enable 2FA:** Activate Two-Factor Authentication on all platforms to safeguard your digital identity. |

---

### ❌ The "Don'ts" (Risk Mitigation)
> **Pro-Tip:** Your digital footprint is permanent. Avoid these common pitfalls to protect your professional credibility.

* 🚩 **Professionalism:** Don't post unprofessional or controversial content that could deter future recruiters.
* 📵 **Privacy:** Don't share sensitive details like your home address, phone number, or student ID.
* 📩 **Networking:** Don't send "spammy" or generic connection requests; always include a personalized note.
* 🚫 **Ethics:** Don't engage in "cyber-flaming" or aggressive public arguments. Maintain a constructive tone.
* 🎣 **Security:** Don't click on suspicious links or "free gift" offers—these are often phishing attempts.

---

### 💡 Final Reflection
> "By adhering to these standards, I aim to build a digital presence that is not only technically proficient but also ethically grounded and secure."
